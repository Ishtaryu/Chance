Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the mobile game Chance (the "App"), developed by ChessWind.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • ❌ No analytics SDK (Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar)
  • ❌ No advertising SDK (AdMob, Unity Ads, or similar)
  • ❌ No third-party SDKs of any kind
  • ❌ No user accounts or registration
  • ❌ No email, phone, or location data collected
  • ❌ No camera, microphone, or sensor access
  • ❌ No tracking or profiling

Local Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device only, using Godot Engine's built-in file system:

  • Player name (the name you choose in-game)
  • High scores and leaderboard entries
  • Language preference (English / Portuguese)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to submit a score to the optional online leaderboard (see below).

Optional Leaderboard

The App includes an optional leaderboard feature that is disabled by default. If you enable it, your chosen player name and score are sent to a server to be displayed publicly alongside other players' scores. No additional information (device ID, IP address, or any identifier) is transmitted.

You can disable the leaderboard feature at any time from within the App — no data is sent while it is turned off.

Internet Permission

The App requests the INTERNET permission solely for the optional leaderboard feature described above. No other network activity occurs.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.
